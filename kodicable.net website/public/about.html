About Us

What We Do

KODI Cable Network is an online streaming service serving users with interests in broadcasting, Emergency Alert Systems, weather, and software development. We host RTMP servers for video and an Icecast server for IP radio, in lists them in a directory reminiscent of cable TV networks (hence our name). Our services are certified "hopefully reliable". KCN is at no cost to our viewers, and currently remains a passion project.

Starting Out

KODI Cable Network started out as a small project in late 2021 as just a simple Google Sites page and a partership with a few very small YouTube channels. As time went on, we made an Icecast server and then we realized we may aswell offer streaming services.

Why?

As stated above, KODI Cable Network is a passion project made for users with interests in broadcasting, Emergency Alert Systems, weather, and software development, so, we made KODI Cable to serve these specific interests and hobbies!

Services

RTMP / HLS Video Streaming

We offer RTMP and HLS video streaming services free of charge for any user who joins us here at KODI Cable Network! We have a fully functional panel for managing your stream. The panel has support for basic features such as viewing your viewer count, viewer stats, changing title / description and more!

Icecast Streaming

As well as offering video streaming services, we also offer audio only streaming services via our Icecast server. This service is also free of charge!

Subprojects

Sometimes we may have subprojects that are not directly related to our main services. These subprojects include various weather and EAS related projects such as our soon to be released WeatherHDS2! These are offered for FREE to our participants and even non-participants!
